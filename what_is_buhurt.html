HOW BUHURT WORKS?

In short, modern Buhurt is a sport where teams of people fight against each other (5vs5, 10vs10, 30vs30..) in full suits of medieval plate armor and blunted weapons, until all of one teams fighters falls down to the ground, gives up or the opposing team has a considerable numerical advantage. The match lasts until one team wins atleast 2 rounds.

SAFETY

Modern Buhurt equipment strikes a perfect balance between medieval authenticity and modern protection. Medieval hand made armor provides great mobility and protection, which is further more enchanced by modern shock absorbing fome padding and great spine protection. Aswell, every tournament has trained marshals who oversee each fight for safety and rules following reasons.

ORGANIZERS/LEAGUES

The two main world wide leagues are Buhurt League (HMB rules set) and MCF (Buhurt with defferent rules set). Links to them are at the end of the page. You might want to check your local communities for any extra organizers/tournaments.
