How Buhurt works?

In short, a modern Buhurt is a sport where teams of people fight against each other (5vs5, 10vs10, 30vs30..) in full suits of medieval plate armor and blunted weapons, until all of one teams fighters falls down to the ground, gives up or the opposing team has a considerable numerical advantage. The fight lasts until one team wins atleast 2 rounds.
